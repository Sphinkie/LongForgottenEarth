THE STORY:

Year 2064

Birth in Paris (EEE- Espace Economique Europeen).



Year 2084

Being always fascinated with spaceships and spacetravels, he joins the Space Exploration Units (SEU), to take part of the human exploration of recently discovered extrasolar planets.



Year 2085

First Encounter between Humans and the alien race of Flexans, and first Interplanery War against the Flexans. The SEU is converted to a military unit, and the scientifics converted into soldiers. After a hard training on the Moon (in the Tycho Brahe Army base), the hero is send for an offensive mission, on bord of the Light Cruiser "Sphinx 177".



Year 2103

As the travel will last 87 years, the whole crew is placed in hibernation stasis. But in middle of the journey the "Sphinx 117" is detected by a Flexan Intercepter. The Flexan launched two missiles and flew. One of the missile is detroyed by the "Sphinx" countermeasures, but the second missile reaches its objective, and seriously damaged the Terran Cruiser.
Uncontrolable and uncontroled, the "Sphinx" drift away thru space.
Years past, centuries past...



Year 30.258

The "Sphinx 117"'s wreck enters an Asteroid Archipel. After some collisions, the only survivor wake up and discover his sad situation...
